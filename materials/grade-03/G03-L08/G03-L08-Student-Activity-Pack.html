A component is a part of a system that plays a specific role. Components can be EXTERNAL (come from outside the system) or INTERNAL (happen within the system).

STEM Challenge: Build a Pollinator Garden Design
ModelIt!
YOUR ENGINEERING MISSION

Design a garden that attracts the most pollinators possible, using the right flowers, colors, and layouts to create a pollinator paradise.

THE SCENARIO
Your school wants to help bees and butterflies by planting a pollinator garden. Your team must design the garden to attract the most pollinators. Use what you learned about pollination to choose the best flowers, arrangement, and features!

Engineering Design Thinking

1. Which flowers will you plant — what colors, shapes, and scents attract pollinators?
2. How will you arrange the flowers — in rows, clusters, or scattered patches?
3. Will you include a water source for pollinators like a shallow dish with pebbles?
4. How will you make sure something is always blooming from spring through fall?
