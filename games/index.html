CAST Science Games

8 fun-first arcade games for Grade 5 science. No quizzes. No lessons. Just play.

Grade 5 · NGSS Aligned · 8 Games
🔥

EMBER

Growth Arcade

You ARE a wildfire. Eat dry fuel to grow massive. Dodge rain clouds. Ride wind gusts. Don't burn out.

L01: When Trees Become Matches
Drought, wind, vegetation & fire spread
🍄

DECOMPOSE

Tower Defense

Place mushrooms, worms, and bacteria to break down falling dead matter before it buries the forest floor.

L02: Nature's Recycling System
Decomposers, moisture, nutrient cycling
☀️

PHOTON

Endless Runner

Race from the sun through the food chain. Each energy transfer drops you to 10%. How far can you go?

L03: Powered by the Sun
Energy transfer, food chains, 10% rule
💧

AQUIFER

Puzzle Strategy

Control the water cycle. Route rain to cities. Only 1% of Earth's water is usable — make it count.

L04: Earth's Hidden Water
Water distribution, evaporation, scarcity
❄️

PHASE SHIFT

Puzzle Platformer

Shift between ice, water, and steam to solve puzzles. Your mass never changes. 25 levels across 5 worlds.

L05: The Disappearing Act
Conservation of mass, states of matter
🌲

CARBON

Idle Growth Sim

Grow a seedling into an ancient giant by catching CO₂ from the air. 95% of a tree's mass comes from air!

L06: Where Does a Tree's Mass Come From?
Photosynthesis, CO₂ absorption, carbon storage
🍄

MYCELIUM

Network Strategy

Build the Wood Wide Web. Connect trees through underground fungal networks to share nutrients and survive.

L07: The Mushroom Network
Mycorrhizal networks, mutualism, ecosystem resilience
🧼

SOAP STRIKE

Arena Action

You're a soap molecule. Ram into viruses tail-first to rip their membranes apart. Build your squad. Survive.

L08: How Soap Destroys Viruses
Amphiphilic molecules, lipid membranes, handwashing
