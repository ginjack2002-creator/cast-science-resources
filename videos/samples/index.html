Research-Based Design Principles Applied

  • Blue's Clues Style: Single warm narrator talking directly to one child, built-in pauses for thinking, 3-clue progressive structure
  • Sesame Street Stickiness: Comprehension drives attention — concepts build on each other, not random segments
  • Gladwell's Stickiness Factor: Simple packaging of complex ideas, narrative format over disconnected facts
  • Nickelodeon Color Science: Warm saturated focal elements (Deep Saffron #F69338, Process Cyan #00BEF2) on calmer backgrounds
  • Voice: Hume "Caring Mother" — warm American female, ~120 wpm, conversational tone, speed 0.85x for young learners
  • Pacing: 60-90 second total runtime, 2-3 key concepts max, natural pauses for child response
